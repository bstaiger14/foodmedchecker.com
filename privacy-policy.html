Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Our privacy approach

Food Med Checker is designed as an educational medication label search tool. We do not sell your information, use advertising trackers, or build personal profiles about visitors.

Information we do not collect

We do not ask you to create an account, submit health records, or provide personal contact information to use the medication food-instruction search.

Medication searches

Medication names entered into the checker are sent to the Food Med Checker API so the site can return FDA labeling information. Do not enter private personal details in the search box.

Cookies and normal technical information

The website may use a small local browser storage setting to remember that you dismissed the cookie notice. Like most websites, basic technical information may be processed by browsers, hosting providers, security tools, and server logs to deliver pages, protect the site, and keep it working.

No sale or advertising tracking

Food Med Checker does not sell visitor information and does not use cookies to track you for advertising.

Questions

If you have questions, please email hello@hellopharmacist.com.
